Khachapuri

Description

This Khachapuri recipe is a combination of fluffy homemade bread, multiple kinds of cheeses and a runny egg in the center. Itâ€™s so cheesy and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Egg
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Combine flour, salt, yeast, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add warm milk.
  3. Using the hook attachment, knead the dough until it takes shape.
  4. Add oil and knead some more.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl. Cover and let it double in size.
  6. Remove from bowl and divide the dough into 4 boat-shaped pieces.
  7. Place the bread boast on a baking sheet lined with parchment.
  8. Combine the three kinds of cheese and fill each bread boat.
  9. Using a pastry brush, brush with egg wash and bake for 15 minutes at 450 degrees Fahrenheit.
  10. Pull the khachapuri out of the oven, then make a well in the middle.
  11. Drop the egg in the center, add pieces of butter to the melted cheese, and bake for another 5-6 minutes.
